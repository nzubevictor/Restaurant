About us

We've been making healthy and tasty food last for 10 years

Glory Kitchen opened in 2012. Glory bears this name as a reference to the owner named Glory Amanda Chigozie. Located in the heart of Lagos, Glory Kitchen encapsulates a relax and casual neighborhood restaurant. Whether you dine at a table in our dinning room, snatch a seat at the bar, Glory Kitchen is the perfect place for great food and hospitality. When you visit our restraunt, you won't believe your eyes with the incredible range of options that are available with us. We specialize in serving more than 50 Nigeria based food. Glory kitchen cook and sell food tray, stew/soup bowls, outdoor catering services. Daily lunch packs, surprise food package.
